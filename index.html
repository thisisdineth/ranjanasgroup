A bout Ranjanas

Ranjanas Facilities Management (RFM)is a well – known Cleaning Maintenance Service. RFM provides exceptional Cleaning and Maintenance services throughout Sri Lanka. We are proud of our reputation as a high-quality cleaning service, and now offer property maintenance services. Our business has grown significantly since our humble beginnings. Since then, we have developed customer loyalty by providing a full range of maintenance and cleaning services. Our customers depend on our ability to exceed expectations to maintain their facilities. Our well-trained staff will cover all aspects of services required for small facilities to large commercial facilities.

Once you try our service, we guarantee you will rely on us for all cleaning and maintenance needs. RFM is committed to customer satisfaction. We will provide you with a well-trained team committed to perfection. In addition, you will be pleased by our reasonable rates. The RFM team is experienced and highly motivated to perform every service to a degree of excellence that our loyal customers expect. Our company has thousands of customers as a result of exceptional teamwork. We are proud of the quality and attention our staff provides to all of our customers.
