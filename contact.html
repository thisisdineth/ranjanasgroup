3 CHEMICALS AND EQUIPMENT RFM use?
We use professional grade equipment and high-quality chemicals to ensure our quality standards are maintained. We use an extensive line of Eco-friendly products, to ensure the safety and health of our employees and community. All employees are specially trained to handle chemicals properly and safely. Extensive Training is provided to ensure all staff members are educated in the proper disposal of all chemicals.
